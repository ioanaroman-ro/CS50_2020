Money Plant

Pilea peperomioides

Pilea peperomioides, also known as Chinese money plant, pancake plant, friendship plant, lefse plant, missionary plant or ufo plant, is a species of flowering plants in the family Urticaceae, native to Yunnan Province in southern China.
