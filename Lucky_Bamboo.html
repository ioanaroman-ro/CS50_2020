Lucky bamboo

Dracaena sanderiana

The Lucky Bamboo is a small bamboo plant, which is believed to bring good luck and fortune in ancient China. This is why this plant is placed in pots in certain parts of the house so that the family or house owner to enjoy the prosperity it brings, according to Feng Shui practices.
